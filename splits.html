What Is a Workout Split?

A workout split is how you divide your training across the week. It helps you balance muscle groups and leave time for recovery.

Choosing a Split

  • Be honest about how many days you can train.
  • Plan at least one rest or light activity day.
  • Use basic movements like squats, presses, and rows.

3-Day Full Body Split

Good for beginners or anyone with a busy schedule.

  1. Day 1: Squats, Push-ups, Lat Pulldowns, Planks
  2. Day 2: Lunges, Bench Press, Rows, Dead Bugs
  3. Day 3: Deadlifts, Shoulder Press, Pull-ups or Assisted Pull-ups, Core Work

4-Day Upper / Lower Split

This split has a bit more volume but still allows recovery time.

Day Focus Example Exercises
Day 1 Upper Body Bench Press, Bent-Over Rows, Push-ups, Bicep Curls
Day 2 Lower Body Squats, Lunges, Romanian Deadlifts, Calf Raises
Day 3 Upper Body Shoulder Press, Lat Pulldowns, Dips or Bench Dips, Tricep Extensions
Day 4 Lower Body Deadlifts, Step-ups, Leg Curls, Core Exercises
